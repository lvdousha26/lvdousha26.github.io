> This post is migrated from [my Luogu blog](https://www.luogu.com.cn/), originally written in 2019.

First time going to an external training course, nervous and excited at the same time. But I was the only one. At Luliang Boutique Hotel, really close to Daming Lake but didn't go — regrettable. My roommates all went sightseeing on day0. They gave us notebooks and pens, plus a camp badge with your name on it.

My roommates were two or three years older. I had just finished 7th grade, and by now they've probably retired from OI and started college. Wonder how they're doing. Didn't keep in touch — didn't even know their names. Not that I forgot; I never knew them in the first place. One roommate was from Linyi Experimental Middle School, attending the provincial team selection class. The other went to a DP/graph theory class. One was going into 11th grade, the other into 10th, I think. What stuck with me was them playing a single-player shooter game in the dorm — not GTA5. Still have no idea what it was. The roommate three years older was playing Imperishable Night and even invited me to join, but I was too bad~ That was 2019, Arknights had just come out, April? The two of them were discussing Arknights in bed.

First lecturer was Zhong Haoxi, then Liang Jiaming, Zhao Hexu. Zhong Haoxi told us STL is slow. Someone took photos of Zhong Haoxi and Zhao Hexu.

zhhx explained that his username was to avoid overlapping with zhx. zhhx has very developed pectoral muscles — can move the left one and the right one independently (found out through QQ space. zhhx even gave a live demonstration).

"Zhao Hefei"

zhhhx talked to us about his dreams.

Ran into zhhx at the train station.

zhx's feed is all lovey-dovey couple posts.

Someone took embarrassing photos of zhx and zhhx.

Back then, the problems in lectures were either from bzoj, loj, poj, or hdu — hard to find on Luogu. Seemed like the teachers didn't have Luogu accounts. A whole new generation of OIers has come along.

Chen Bo sat to my left. He later became my senior at school.

Back then COVID didn't exist yet...

Got 3rd place in the mock contest and won a keyboard.
